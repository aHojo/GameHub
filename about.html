About

GameHub serves as a central hub for both gamers and non-gamers an instantaneous guide to learn more about all games available on Steam. Created by four students in the Penn LPS Bootcamp, GameHub's central goal is to provide users with something visually stimulating to gain a better understanding of specific games. It provides what's necessary, and limits the wordy descriptions.


Audience

Anyone 18+ (due to age restrictions on particular games)

Created By
Andrew Hojonowski, Cong Nguyen, Megan Pellegrino-Zubricky, and Justin Verterano
